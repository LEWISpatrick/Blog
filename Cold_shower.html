Cold shower 101 💪

Start Gradually:

If you're new to cold showers, don't plunge headfirst into an icy stream. Begin by incorporating brief cold intervals at the end of your regular shower. Gradually decrease the temperature until you build the resilience to fully embrace the cold.

Embrace the Shock:

The initial shock of cold water hitting your skin might feel intense, but Arnold would tell you that it's a crucial part of the experience. Embrace the shock, take deep breaths, and let your body adapt. Remember, discomfort is temporary, but the benefits are lasting.

Gradual Temperature Decrease:

For a full cold shower experience, gradually decrease the water temperature during your shower. Start with warm water and, step by step, turn the dial towards the colder side. This gradual shift allows your body to adjust and avoids a sudden shock to your system.
